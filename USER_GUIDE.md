# Luoo MP3 Player - User Guide

Welcome to the Luoo MP3 Player! This guide will help you navigate the application and enjoy the music.

## Browsing Journals

The main page displays a grid of music journals (volumes).

-   **Navigation**: Use the pagination controls at the top right to move between pages. You can click the arrows (`<`, `>`) or type a page number directly into the box.
-   **Filtering by Tags**:
    -   The sidebar on the left lists all available tags (genres, moods, etc.).
    -   Click a tag to filter the journals.
    -   You can select multiple tags to refine your search.
    -   Selected tags appear at the top of the page. Click the `×` on a tag to remove it, or click "Clear all" to reset.
    -   **Pinned Tags**: "All Tags" and "No Tag" are pinned to the top of the sidebar for easy access.

## Playing Music

There are several ways to start listening:

1.  **Play a Volume**: Hover over any journal card cover image and click the large **Play (▶)** button that appears. This will start playing the first song of that volume and queue the rest.
2.  **Play a Specific Song**:
    -   Go to a Volume Detail page.
    -   Click on any song in the playlist to play it immediately.

## Viewing Volume Details

To see more information about a journal:

1.  **Click the Title**: Click the title text below the cover image on the main card list.
2.  **Click the Details Button**: Hover over a journal card cover image and click the **arrow icon (↗)** in the top-right corner.

On the Detail Page, you will see:
-   **Top**: The Journal Title and Tags.
-   **Middle**:
    -   **Left**: The Cover Image.
    -   **Right**: The Playlist (Tracklist). You can scroll through the songs here.
-   **Bottom**: The full Journal Description. This area is scrollable if the text is long.

## Player Controls

The player bar is located at the bottom of the screen and persists as you navigate.

-   **Play/Pause**: Toggle playback.
-   **Previous/Next (Play/Skip)**: Skip to the previous or next track.
-   **Progress Bar**: Drag to seek within the song.
-   **Volume**: Adjust the volume slider.
-   **Playlist Toggle**: Click the list icon on the right to open the current play queue. You can click songs in this queue to jump to them.
